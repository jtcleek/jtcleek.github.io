Hey there, I'm Joshua Cleek, reaching out from the heart of the Kansas City metro. By day, I'm donning the hat of a Solutions Delivery Engineer in the healthcare industry, diving deep into the fascinating worlds of Linux, automation and virtualization.

My life is a delightful mix of tech wizardry and everyday pleasures. When I'm not deciphering the intricacies of technology, you'll find me zipping through the streets on my bicycle, cheering for my favorite football team, and embracing the joys of fitness. And let's not forget my love for the finer things – beer and whiskey.

Now, let's talk quirks, because why not? I've turned the art of research into a superpower, especially when it comes to life's most mundane purchases. Toothpaste or a toaster, I've got the lowdown on it all. When it comes to home comfort, I march to the beat of a frugal drum. The thermostat in my house? A cool 60 degrees in the winter. Why? Because I value saving a few bucks over cozy warmth. And here's the kicker – I operate on less than 6 hours of sleep regularly. Call it dedication or call it madness; I'm living proof that you can achieve great things even with a bit of sleep deprivation.

Why the blog? I'm on a mission to leave a digital footprint that motivates and inspires. Join me on this journey where technology meets passion, frugality, and a touch of sleep-deprived determination.
